Meet the Creator

Emmy [she/they] is creating content, writing articles, and the voice behind The Dyke Agenda.

I created The Dyke Agenda as an outlet for my thoughts, feelings, and fleeting jokes surrounding dyke culture. Hailing from the Chicagoland area, I view dyke culture through a lived experience as a Black non-binary lesbian; an identity and experience that is often erased and/or left out of mainstream media. Through this blog, I hope to shine a light on some of the most beloved aspects of dyke culture: the good, the bad, and the hilarious.

thedykeagenda is a hub for lesbian media & culture, organized + written by emmy jordan. by dykes, for dykes.
